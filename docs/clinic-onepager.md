# OduDoc — Clinic-in-a-box

**One platform. Every patient. Zero monthly fee.**

OduDoc is a telemedicine + electronic medical-records platform built for doctors who want to run their own practice — online or in-clinic — without paying for separate appointment software, EMR licences, prescription tools, or invoicing systems.

---

## What you get, free

### 🩺 Clinic EMR
Patients · SOAP visit notes · Lab reports & scans · Invoices · Medical certificates. Searchable history, FHIR + HL7 export, audit trail. Up to **50 new patients per month free**.

### 💊 Prescriptions
23 professional templates · AI prescription assistant · voice dictation in **90+ languages** · drug-interaction red flags · auto-saved patient history.

### 👥 Multi-staff team
Free tier: **1 nurse + 1 front desk seat**, with role-based permissions. Front desk registers patients, nurse logs visits, you sign certificates.

### 📋 Medical certificates
Sick leave · fitness to work · fitness to travel · vaccination · general. Every certificate includes a public verification URL HR can use to confirm authenticity.

### 💳 Patient online payments
Auto-generated payment links. Patient pays online, you see "paid" instantly on the invoice.

### 🌍 Cross-border ready
Country-aware: Indian doctors see ₹ everywhere, US doctors $, UK doctors £. India-licensed doctors are correctly restricted to Indian patients (per IMC telemedicine guidelines).

### 🇮🇳 ABDM-compatible *(India)*
ABHA Health ID linking + HPR doctor registry support. National Digital Health Mission integration scaffolding ready — patients with ABHA can sync their records to any compliant app.

## Get started

**For solo doctors:**
1. Apply at `https://www.odudoc.com/for-doctors`
2. Upload license + ID — verified within 48 hours
3. Set your fee, your hours, your specialty

1. The clinic owner applies first (becomes the admin)
2. Owner adds nurses / front desk / additional doctors at `/dashboard/doctor/emr/staff`
